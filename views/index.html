Timestamp Microservice

Gives you Unix and natural timestamp from the provided Unix timestamp or natural timestamp (e.g. March 20, 2001)

Example use:

  • Natural: https://the-timestamp-micro.glitch.me/March%2020,%202001
  • Unix: https://the-timestamp-micro.glitch.me/985046400000

Example output

{
  unix: 985046400000
  natural: "March 20, 2001"
}
